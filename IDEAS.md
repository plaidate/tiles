# Tiles — game ideas

● shipped ○ open ✕ tried and cut

- ● **blast** — Bomberman-like single-screen arcade: pillar lattice,
  crates, chain bombs, crank fuse dial.
- ● **spirit** — KiKi KaiKai-like 4-way shooter: yokai waves, lantern
  cover, crank-banked wand sweep.
- ● **relic** — Zelda-like screen-flip RPG: 10 rooms, keys/doors,
  boomerang, boss, datastore save/continue, crank spin attack.
- ○ **turbo** — top-down single-screen racer (Super Sprint): crank =
  steering wheel, tile-defined tracks, oil slicks.
- ● **burrow** — Boulder-Dash-style digger on a scrolling 40x28 cave:
  carve-away dirt (Map.set repaint), falling/rolling rocks, gem quota,
  crank pans the camera. First game on the Cam module.
- ○ **keep** — single-screen tower defense on the tile grid: crank
  aims a ballista, enemies path via Phys.bfs.
- ○ **freight** — Sokoban with a clock: push crates onto pads before
  the train leaves; crank scrubs a level timer.
- ○ **haunt** — Pac-Man-shaped maze chase with the corner-assist
  movement; crank = one rechargeable ghost-freeze.
